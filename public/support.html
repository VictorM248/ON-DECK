ON-DECK Support

ON-DECK is an internal queue management application for Dalton Corp automotive dealership employees only.

Contact Support

For technical issues or questions about the app, please contact your administrator:

vmacias@daltoncorp.com

Common Issues

Can't sign in? ON-DECK uses Microsoft SSO. Make sure you're signing in with your @daltoncorp.com email address. Contact your administrator if you don't have access.

Not showing in the queue? Your account may not be assigned to a store yet. Contact your administrator to get set up.

Not receiving notifications? Make sure notifications are enabled for ON-DECK in your iPhone Settings.

About

ON-DECK is developed and maintained by Dalton Corp. This app is intended exclusively for Dalton Corp employees.
